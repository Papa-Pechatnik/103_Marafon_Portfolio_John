How to paint a portrait with vibrant colours with Maria Poliakova

We understand it can be difficult working with colors and lighting, so here are some insights from the talented and lovely Maria Poliakova. Maria is an artist from Kiev who sometimes goes a little crazy with the colors, to great effect.
In this tutorial, Maria gives a step by step process of creating a colourful portrait using her Wacom Cintiq Pro 13. She´s excited to share with you some tips, explanations and technical details, so you too can learn to draw a portrait with vibrant colours, make colour adjustments and organize your workspace.

This Series of Abstract Portrait Paintings is Surreal and Bold

References: erikolson & inspirationhut.net

Finding unique abstract art has become a challenge with the recent explosion of anything different and out-of-the-ordinary, but that’s not the case for this stunning abstract portrait painting series.
Using a background of bright block colors, Canadian artist Erik Olson’s collection of abstract portrait paintings uses experimental techniques to give layers and depth to the portraits he paints. Some of his pieces are simple and almost recognizable, while others are much more abstract making it harder to make out the features on the face.
Painting his faces using rectangular shapes, Erik’s interesting mix of surrealist and realistic work has received the publicity it deserves—the artist has had several exhibitions and has won several awards for his work.
